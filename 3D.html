3D shooter


This is my first good game that I am proud of. The assignment was to make a 3D game with Unity. I chose to make a shooter. You spawn in the middle of the map with a gun, knife and hand grenades. You must climb up the mountain and defeat the enemy at the top of the mountain as quickly as possible. Once you have done that, new enemies stop coming and you can finish them all off. I did use assist with this task by making everything a little easier.
